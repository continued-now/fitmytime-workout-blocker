Features

Everything you need
to stay consistent

Built for busy people who want to stay fit without spending time planning every workout.

📅

Smart Calendar Scheduling

Automatically scans your Google Calendar for free time slots and creates personalized workout events during your preferred windows. Deduplicates to prevent double-booking.

📅
⏱

Live Countdown

Real-time timer to your next workout. Pulses when it's go time and auto-expands exercises.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build daily streaks with milestones at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days. Track your all-time best.

🏆

Achievement Badges

Unlock badges for your first workout, 50 completions, 100 minutes, and streak milestones.

💪

Personal Records & Progressive Overload

Automatically tracks your best sets, reps, and weight per exercise. When you've been consistent at the same weight, get smart suggestions to level up.

💪
🕒

Flexible Rescheduling

Can't make it? Reschedule to a new slot or skip. Life happens — FitMyTime adapts.

🗓️

Rate & Reflect

Rate intensity 1–5 and add notes after each session. Influences future workout suggestions.
