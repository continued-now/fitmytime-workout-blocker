Description

FitMyTime is a free Chrome extension that automatically schedules personalized workouts into your Google Calendar. No more excuses — if you have a free slot, you have time to exercise.

How It Works

  • Connect your Google Calendar with one click
  • Set your fitness goal, preferred days, time windows, and available equipment
  • FitMyTime scans your calendar daily and fills free slots with tailored workouts
  • Complete workouts, track your streaks, and watch your progress grow

Key Features

  • Smart Scheduling — Finds gaps in your calendar and creates workout events with exercises, sets, and reps
  • Live Countdown Timer — See a real-time countdown to your next workout right in the popup
  • Streak Tracking — Build daily streaks with milestone celebrations at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days
  • Post-Workout Rating — Rate intensity (1-5) and add notes after each workout to influence future suggestions
  • Achievement Badges — Unlock badges for first workout, 5/10/50 completions, 100 minutes, and streak milestones
  • Personal Records — Track your best sets, reps, and weight per exercise with automatic PR detection
  • Monthly Calendar View — See your workout history at a glance on a visual monthly calendar
  • Flexible Rescheduling — Can’t make it? Reschedule to a new time or skip with one click
  • Exercise Swap — Don’t like an exercise? Swap it for an alternative targeting the same muscle group
  • Custom Exercises — Add your own exercises to the library for more personalized workouts
  • Multiple Calendars — Choose which Google Calendar to add workout events to
  • Notification Preferences — Get reminded 5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes before each workout
  • Export History — Download your complete workout history as a CSV file
  • Progressive Overload Hints — Get suggestions to increase weight when you’ve been consistent
  • Weekly Goal Progress — Set a weekly workout target and track your progress with a visual bar

Privacy First

FitMyTime stores all data locally on your device using Chrome’s built-in storage. Your workout history, preferences, and calendar data never leave your browser. We don’t have servers, accounts, or analytics.

Works With

  • Google Chrome, Brave, Microsoft Edge (Chromium-based browsers)
  • Google Calendar (personal or workspace)
